who i am

The honest version.

I'm Muhim — a 20-year-old based in California who spends most of his time asking "why does this work like that?" and then going down a rabbit hole to find out.

Tech is my playground. I'm the person people call when something's broken and nobody knows why. I've learned more from troubleshooting random issues than from any class. I find that kind of problem-solving satisfying in a way I can't fully explain.

I care about personal finance in a practical sense — not get-rich-quick stuff, but understanding how money actually works and making smarter decisions with what I have. Same goes for health. Nothing extreme, just doing the basics consistently.

I won't pretend I have it all figured out. I overthink sometimes. Discipline is a work in progress. But I show up, I figure things out, and I keep moving.
